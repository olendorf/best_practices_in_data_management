## What Is Data?

<div class="fragment">
    <ul>
        <li>
            Factual information (such as measurements or statistics) 
            used as a basis for reasoning or calculation
        </li>
        <li>
            Information in digital form that can be transmitted or processed
        </li>
        <li>
            Stuff you can do math on
        </li>
    </ul>
        

</div>
